Neal McKee is a freelance photographer and retoucher who currently resides in Duesseldorf, Germany. He was born in Southern Germany and pursued fashion photography in Stuttgart, Berlin and Paris for a year before beginning his studies in Frankfurt.

In 2011 he enrolled at the Duesseldorf Art Academy where he studied with Rosemarie Trockel and now Marcel Odenbach.
